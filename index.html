Three kinds of soccer

TypeWho playsExample
Club leagueCity / club teams, full seasonPremier League, La Liga, MLS
Club cupClubs (often best from many countries)Champions League, Europa League
Country cupNational teams (countries / flags)World Cup, Euros, Copa America

Club competitions run most of the year. Country cups are big events every 2-4 years (plus qualifiers and Nations League in between).
